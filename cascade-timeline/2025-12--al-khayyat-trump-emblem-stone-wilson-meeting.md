In late 2025, days or weeks before the FY 2026 NDAA conference committee finalized Caesar Act repeal language, **Mohamad Al-Khayyat** — one of the three Syrian-born Qatari billionaire brothers controlling Power International Holding — returned to Washington, D.C. carrying **a special stone carved with the Trump family emblem**, celebrating the Al-Khayyat brothers' proposed Trump-branded luxury golf course in Syria. He presented the stone to **Rep. Joe Wilson (R-SC) in the congressman's Capitol Hill office** for onward delivery to the White House.

The episode is the single most operationally concrete documentation of how Trump-family-adjacency was converted into sanctions-policy leverage during the Caesar Act repeal lobbying campaign.

## What Happened

Per subsequent New York Times reporting (April 2026, surfaced through MSNBC Maddow Blog, Syrian Observer, Hoodline, and other secondary outlets):

1. **Rep. Wilson advised Al-Khayyat** that "plans for a luxury golf course carry the Trump Organization brand as a way of getting the American president's attention"
2. **Mohamad Al-Khayyat returned to Washington** "late last year" carrying the carved stone
3. **The stone bore the Trump family emblem** and celebrated the proposed Syrian golf course
4. **Al-Khayyat presented the stone to Rep. Wilson** in the congressman's Capitol Hill office "to deliver to the White House"
5. **Al-Khayyat then joined additional lobbying meetings** with "at least 12 members of Congress"
6. **Weeks later, Caesar Act repeal** was bundled into the FY 2026 NDAA and signed into law by Trump on December 18, 2025

The New York Times characterized the episode as exemplary of "a warped system of executive patronage."

## Why This Event Matters

This is the **most specific publicly-documented instance** of a foreign private-capital counterparty converting Trump-family-brand-leverage into U.S. legislative action. Three structural features distinguish it:

1. **The brand-leverage strategy was explicitly coached by a member of Congress** — Rep. Wilson's recommendation that the golf course "carry the Trump Organization brand as a way of getting the American president's attention" is on the public record via NYT reporting
2. **The commemorative stone functioned as a direct personal gift with symbolic value** — designed not as commercial marketing but as a signaling object intended for the Oval Office
3. **The temporal proximity to NDAA passage is tight** — the stone delivery preceded the FY 2026 NDAA conference-committee finalization by weeks at most, with the repeal passing the Senate 77-20 and signed December 18, 2025

## Thomas Barrack Context

Per the Maddow Blog account, **Thomas Barrack — Trump's Special Envoy to Syria** — provided "backing" for the Al-Khayyat brothers' broader Syria commercial expansion. Barrack is a long-time Trump associate who faced (and was acquitted in) a federal prosecution for foreign-agent activity on behalf of the UAE during Trump 1. His Special Envoy role in Trump 2 places him at the diplomatic interface between the U.S. government and the post-Assad Syrian transitional government — the same counterparty awarding $12B in reconstruction contracts to Power International Holding.

## Kushner / Ivanka Real-Estate Context

Per the same reporting, the Al-Khayyat brothers were **simultaneously negotiating a real-estate partnership with Jared Kushner and Ivanka Trump** for the Sazan Island / Zvërnec Albania project. The NYT's April 19, 2026 Sazan Island reveal (see [[2026-04-19--nyt-reveals-sazan-island-qatar-al-khayyat-brothers]]) documented this Albania-project counterparty relationship. The stone-delivery episode therefore occurred while **three separate Trump-family commercial engagements** with the Al-Khayyat brothers were active in parallel:

1. Proposed Trump-branded Syrian golf course (via Trump Organization licensing)
2. Sazan Island Albania resort (via Kushner Affinity + Ivanka Atlantic Incubation Partners)
3. Barrack backing of broader Syria commercial expansion (via Special Envoy diplomatic support)

## Why This Matters for the Capture-Cascade Frame

The stone-delivery event is the single clearest artifact of the **Trump-Brand-as-Sanctions-Policy-Leverage** pattern. Prior Trump-family commercial-to-governmental-favor events (Kushner $2B Saudi PIF investment, QIA $623M Park Lane, Tahnoon $500M WLFI) each involved complex corporate transactions that required extensive unpacking. The carved stone is simple: a physical object, carried to Capitol Hill, designed to be delivered to the White House, presented by a member of Congress as a favor to a foreign billionaire seeking U.S. legislative action.

The episode will likely feature in any future prosecutorial or congressional-oversight reconstruction of the Caesar Act repeal lobbying campaign because:

- It is physically documentable
- Rep. Wilson is a public-record participant
- The NYT has reported it
- The commercial benefit ($12B in Syria contracts) is quantifiable
- The foreign state (Qatar, via PIH) is identifiable
