Cantor Opportunities LLC signs sovereign wealth fund referral agreement with Urramoor Limited, a company controlled by Prince Andrew, Duke of York. The agreement established a 50/50 fee split for introducing sovereign wealth fund contacts to U.S. real estate deals brokered by Cantor Fitzgerald.

Jeffrey Epstein dictated the final terms of the agreement; documents reached his inbox within hours of completion, indicating his central role in facilitating the arrangement between Prince Andrew and Howard Lutnick's firm. The deal formalized the intersection of royal access to Gulf sovereign wealth funds with Cantor Fitzgerald's financial infrastructure, creating a channel through which Epstein could leverage both relationships simultaneously.
