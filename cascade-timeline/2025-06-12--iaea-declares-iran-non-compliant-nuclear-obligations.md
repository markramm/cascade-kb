The IAEA Board of Governors passed a resolution declaring Iran in breach of nuclear non-proliferation obligations - the first such finding since 2005. The vote was 19 in favor, 3 against, and 11 abstentions. The resolution cited Iran's failures since 2019 to provide "full and timely cooperation regarding undeclared nuclear material and activities." In response, Iran announced plans to establish a new enrichment site and replace first-generation centrifuges at Fordow with advanced sixth-generation machines. The finding came one day before Israel launched major military strikes on Iran's nuclear facilities.
