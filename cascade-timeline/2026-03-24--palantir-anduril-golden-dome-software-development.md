Palantir and Anduril Industries are jointly developing the core software for the Golden Dome missile defense system — a $185 billion space-based shield designed to intercept ballistic, cruise, and hypersonic missiles. The companies are building a command-and-control platform that processes data from distributed sensors and enables commanders to coordinate weapons systems against aerial threats in real time.

The project also involves Elon Musk's SpaceX (satellite launch and Starshield constellation), along with traditional defense primes Lockheed Martin, RTX, and Northrop Grumman. Additional participants include Aalyria Technologies, Scale AI, and Swoop Technologies.

Trump signed the Golden Dome executive order in January 2025 with an initial $175 billion estimate. The Department of War subsequently increased the budget to $185 billion to accelerate space-based capabilities.
