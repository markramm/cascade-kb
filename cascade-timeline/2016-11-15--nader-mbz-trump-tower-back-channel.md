In mid-November 2016, immediately after Trump's election victory, George Nader — Lebanese-American businessman documented as the **primary UAE-Saudi conduit to the Trump campaign and transition** per the Mueller record and the cascade [[nader-george]] actor entry — began operating an MBZ-aligned back channel to the Trump transition team. The November-December 2016 contacts included multiple meetings at Trump Tower involving Nader, Erik Prince, Steve Bannon, and ultimately Jared Kushner, and culminated in the **January 11, 2017 Seychelles meeting** between Erik Prince and Russian banker Kirill Dmitriev that the Mueller investigation documented.

Per the Abramson-thesis arc framework ([[epstein-kushner-gulf-sovereign-18-year-arc-abramson-thesis]]), this period is the operational opening of Phase 2 — the **transfer of Phase 1 Gulf-sovereign cultivation from Epstein-as-broker to Kushner-as-principal-counterparty**. Whether the transfer ran through Epstein-coordinated introduction (the strong-form Abramson reading) or through parallel cultivation (the weaker reading where Nader/Prince operated independently of Epstein but inside the same network), the structural point is that **the foreign-side principals — MBZ, MBS, the Al Thanis — did not change**. The U.S.-side counterparty did.

The October 17, 2018 Ruemmler-to-Epstein photograph of Nader with MBS at 2:20 AM (EFTA02618825) and the June 2019 Bannon-Epstein exchange after Nader's child-pornography arrest ("MBZ must have coughed him up" — EFTA01617920) confirm that the Nader channel and the Epstein channel were operationally connected through 2018-2019. The 2016-2017 transition period is when the connection was most operationally active for U.S.-policy purposes, and the transition contacts established the Kushner-MBS / Kushner-MBZ rapport that would manifest in May 2017 (Riyadh trip), October 2017 (the Ritz-Carlton intelligence-sharing visit), and August 2018 (the QIA-Brookfield 666 Fifth bailout).
