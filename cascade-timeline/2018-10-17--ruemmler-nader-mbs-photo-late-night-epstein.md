Per Exhibit EFTA02618825 from the 2025 House Oversight Epstein document production, on October 17, 2018, attorney Kathryn Ruemmler — former White House Counsel under President Obama, then a partner at Latham & Watkins, later Goldman Sachs General Counsel — sent Jeffrey Epstein a photograph of George Nader with Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman at **2:20 AM**.

The exhibit is structurally significant for the Phase 1-3 overlap thesis ([[epstein-kushner-gulf-sovereign-18-year-arc-abramson-thesis]]) on multiple grounds:

1. **Intelligence-sharing tempo, not casual forwarding.** A 2:20 AM photo transmission to a financial-intelligence broker is not the communication pattern of a personal acquaintance forwarding an interesting picture. The timing is consistent with the operational tempo of intelligence-cycle distribution — share-as-it-arrives rather than share-when-convenient. The exhibit documents that Ruemmler was operating at intelligence-distribution tempo with Epstein on Gulf-principal matters.

2. **The photo subject (Nader with MBS) is the operational core.** The two principals captured in the photograph are the structural backbone of the UAE-Saudi-Trump-channel architecture — Nader as the documented primary UAE-Saudi conduit ([[nader-george]]), MBS as the operational Saudi principal whom Kushner had developed personal rapport with by this point ([[2017-11-01--kushner-shares-classified-intel-leading-to-saudi-a]]). The photograph is a relationship-status update on the operational network.

3. **Ruemmler-as-cross-network liaison.** Ruemmler's role is among the most structurally distinctive in the Epstein documentary record. As former White House Counsel, partner at Latham & Watkins (Epstein's primary law firm during the cultivation period), and subsequent Goldman Sachs General Counsel, Ruemmler occupied seats at the intersection of executive-branch legal infrastructure, Epstein's personal legal infrastructure, and major-bank counterparty infrastructure. The October 2018 photo transmission documents that Ruemmler was active in the Gulf-coordination network at the same intelligence-sharing tempo as Bannon ([[2018-03-01--sulayem-bannon-barak-epstein-71st-street-meeting]]) and Epstein himself.

The October 2018 timing is also significant: the photo arrives **two weeks after the Khashoggi assassination** (October 2, 2018), during the period when Kushner was advising MBS on weathering the international response, and **ten weeks after the QIA-Brookfield 666 Fifth bailout closed** (August 3, 2018). The exhibit places Ruemmler-Epstein-Nader-MBS coordination active during the most operationally significant week of the Phase 3 capital-operationalization window.
