On October 3, 2025, DHS Secretary Kristi Noem visited the U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement processing facility in Broadview, Illinois — a western Chicago suburb where immigration protesters had maintained a sustained presence — and delivered a pep talk to agents as they prepared to leave for enforcement operations. Her remarks, recorded by conservative influencer Benny Johnson who was embedded with agents, included the statement: "So today, when we leave here, we're going to go hard. We're going to hammer these guys that are advocating for violence against the American people."

Noem made no comments to Chicago-area journalists present at the facility. The visit coincided with Operation Midway Blitz, a mass immigration enforcement sweep in the Chicago metro area in which Greg Bovino — then serving as operational commander — and his agents detained protesters outside the Broadview facility. CNN, NBC News, and WGN-TV documented the protests and multiple protester arrests on the day of the visit; Illinois Governor Pritzker condemned Noem's visit and called for her accountability.

WBEZ Chicago subsequently published a full transcript in November 2025 of how Noem and Bovino together defended the aggressive enforcement posture, documenting both the operational direction and the rhetorical frame they advanced publicly. The "go hard / hammer these guys" speech is the clearest single instance of Noem setting the tone of force directly before enforcement operations — not as policy abstraction but as an instruction to agents preparing to deploy.

Structurally, the speech illustrates how the command-and-control inversion enabled by the commander-at-large designation operated in practice: a cabinet secretary bypassing the CBP chain of command to deliver operational instruction directly to agents, with a loyalist commander present to execute. The Broadview pep talk is the Ch2 title image because it makes visible what is normally administrative: the translation of political will into kinetic force at the moment of departure.
