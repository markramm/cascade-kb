Rep. Robin Kelly (D-IL) filed articles of impeachment against DHS Secretary Kristi Noem following the ICE shooting of US citizen Renee Nicole Good in Minneapolis. The three articles charged: (1) willfully obstructing congressional oversight and withholding funds, (2) compromising due process, and (3) abusing office for personal benefit. Multiple co-sponsors included Reps. Maxine Dexter (OR), Emily Randall (WA), Yassamin Ansar (AZ), and Sydney Kamlager-Dove (CA). Rep. Kevin Mullin (CA) separately called for impeachment, while Rep. Brad Schneider (IL), chair of the moderate New Democrat Coalition, demanded Noem resign or be removed. The effort followed Noem's controversial response to the shooting, in which she called Good's actions 'a domestic act of terrorism' and claimed Good was 'weaponizing' her vehicle—claims contradicted by video evidence according to Minneapolis officials. Rep. Jared Moskowitz criticized Noem's 'penchant for playing dress-up,' displaying photos of her in various costumes during operations.
