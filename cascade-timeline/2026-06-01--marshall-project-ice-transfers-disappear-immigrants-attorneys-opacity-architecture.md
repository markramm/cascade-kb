The Marshall Project published an investigation on June 1, 2026 documenting that ICE's detainee transfer practices have dramatically escalated under Trump's second term: the number of people transferred five or more times tripled between Biden's final year and Trump's first year back; out-of-state transfers within 24 hours more than doubled, with ICE transferring nearly 41,700 people to other states within 24 hours in Trump's first year. The investigation documents a named case — Abdullahi Mohamed, detained in Massachusetts, then transferred across Mississippi, Louisiana, and Texas within five days before deportation to Somalia — illustrating the mechanism: transfer velocity makes habeas corpus petitions effectively impossible to file before deportation occurs. ICE's own policy requires senior official sign-off and notification "as soon as practicable" within 24 hours when a detainee has an attorney or pending case — a policy attorneys describe as routinely ignored. The investigation further documents that transfer destination is not random: ICE strategically moves detainees toward facilities in the 5th Circuit (covering three states that hold 70% of the nation's largest detention facilities), where the appeals court is most favorable to deportation orders.

The transfer-opacity mechanism is structurally distinct from detention-conditions and detention-deaths and is the missing logistical substrate link in the detention-pipeline architecture: it functions as the operational layer that prevents legal intervention before deaths occur and before voluntary-departure coercion can be contested in court. This connects directly to the coercive-conditions investigation published by The Marshall Project on May 30 ([[2026-05-30--marshall-project-ice-coercive-voluntary-departure-conditions-sevenfold-surge]]) — transfer-opacity is the logistics layer; coercion-through-conditions is the enforcement layer; together they produce the sevenfold voluntary-departure surge documented at [[2026-05-08--washington-post-voluntary-departure-orders-80000-detention-coercion]]. The combination of opacity-transfer + conditions-coercion + 5th-Circuit forum-shopping constitutes a three-layer detention architecture designed to neutralize legal intervention before it can reach a court. Over 13,500 people were deported following the same pattern as Mohamed.
