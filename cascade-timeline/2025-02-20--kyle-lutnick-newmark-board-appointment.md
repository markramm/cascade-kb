Kyle Lutnick (28) is appointed director of Newmark Group Inc. and Executive Vice Chairman of Cantor Fitzgerald as his father Howard Lutnick departs the firms to serve as Commerce Secretary in the Trump administration. Brandon Lutnick (27) simultaneously becomes CEO and Chairman of Cantor Fitzgerald.

The rapid transfer of control over major financial firms to two men in their twenties with limited independent track records raised immediate conflict-of-interest concerns. Senators Elizabeth Warren and Mark Raskin later described Newmark as "now owned by his sons," highlighting the thin line between Howard Lutnick's government role shaping trade and financial policy and his children's control of the firms that stand to benefit from those policies.
