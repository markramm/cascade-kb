The **Mathias Corvinus Collegium (MCC) Feszt 2025** in Esztergom, Hungary (opening July 31, 2025; multi-day) concentrated the trans-Atlantic integralist-illiberal network at a single venue funded by dividends from Russian oil. Three documented events at the festival are load-bearing for the investigation:

**1. Orr interviewed Thiel on stage.** Cambridge philosopher and JD Vance "British sherpa" James Orr served as the on-stage interviewer for Peter Thiel at the MCC Feszt — a structured, substantive working relationship, not merely coincidental attendance. (*DeSmog*, August 7, 2025; confirmed by subsequent research aggregation.) The MCC Feszt on-stage interview between Orr and Thiel resolves the tick-28 "co-attendance" framing: this is documented **host-interviewer / interviewee collaboration** in a state-funded Hungarian venue.

**2. Orr-Deneen-Balázs Orbán panel: "Beyond Liberalism – Reclaiming Politics for the National Interest."** On July 31, Orr co-paneled with **Patrick Deneen** (Notre Dame; *Why Liberalism Failed*), **Balázs Orbán** (Hungarian PM's political director; MCC chair), and Zoltán Szalai (MCC Director General). This is the **first documented direct face-to-face working appearance between James Orr and Patrick Deneen** in the research corpus — resolving a prior research gap. Orr characterized Hungary as "a shining City upon a Hill" and praised its parliament as "the supermodel of all parliaments." Deneen: "Europe needs its own George Washington." Balázs Orbán demanded von der Leyen's resignation over the EU-US trade framework.

**3. Orr's "Ukraine brain" remarks.** Orr attacked the Western framing of Russia's Ukraine invasion as "naive and dangerous" — characterizing the Hitler/Churchill analogy as a "peculiar psychological condition" he called "Ukraine brain." He described Russia's invasion as a "regional Slavic conflict" he doesn't "care very much about" and praised Hungary's pro-diplomatic-caution stance as "exceptional." This was delivered at a festival whose main stage was **sponsored by MOL Group** — Hungary's national oil company, which refines Russian oil throughout the conflict.

**Other confirmed MCC Feszt 2025 speakers**: Viktor Orbán; Sebastian Kurz (former Austrian chancellor); Dominic Cummings (former UK Downing Street adviser); Ayaan Hirsi Ali (Hoover Institution); Alice Weidel (AfD leader, Germany); Michael Shellenberger; Zsolt Hernádi (MOL Group CEO). Expected attendance: ~50,000.
