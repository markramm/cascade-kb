On May 25, 2026, **Anthropic co-founder Chris Olah appeared at Pope Leo XIV's side at the Vatican Synod Hall** for the release of the encyclical [[2026-05-25--pope-leo-xiv-magnifica-humanitas-encyclical-disarm-ai-concentration-tech-power|*Magnifica Humanitas*]], jointly calling for a **Church-tech ethics partnership** to build AI safeguards. Olah — a 33-year-old AI-lab leader described as an atheist — told the gathering: **"We need informed critics who will tell the labs when we are failing... We need moral voices that the incentives cannot bend."** The Washington Post headlined the event **"Anthropic aligns with Vatican over White House."** The encyclical itself urges governments, corporations, and individuals to **slow the rate of AI development** and keep it **subject to ethical and political oversight** (WaPo, NCR, RNS).

**Structural significance — the AI-safety wing of the industry publicly sides with magisterial restraint against the administration's deregulation posture.** Two threads converge here:

1. **The next beat in the Anthropic-vs-administration arc.** This is not Anthropic's first break with the Trump administration over AI safety. The KB documents the prior sequence: Hegseth's blacklist threat ([[2026-02-24--hegseth-threatens-anthropic-blacklist-military-ai-access]]), Anthropic's refusal of the Pentagon ultimatum over its red lines ([[2026-02-26--anthropic-rejects-pentagon-ultimatum-red-lines]]), Trump's "supply-chain risk" blacklist designation ([[2026-02-27--trump-blacklists-anthropic-supply-chain-risk-designation]]), and Anthropic's suit against DOD ([[2026-03-06--anthropic-sues-dod-supply-chain-risk-designation]]). The Vatican alignment extends that conflict from the procurement/military domain into the religious/AI-ethics domain: the same company the administration blacklisted for holding AI red-lines now stands beside the Pope advancing them.

2. **The "two luddites" of the legitimation layer, allied in public.** [[crypto-christology-as-legitimation-layer]] documents Thiel's Antichrist-lecture inversion in which **AI-safety advocates** and **"the popes who teach AI ethics"** are cast as the luddite/katechon — the lawless force whose restraint of technology must itself be restrained. On this stage, both of those named targets — an AI-safety lab and the Pope — stood together. It is the precise inverse of the framing the tech-right legitimation layer requires, and the direct counter to [[2025-02-11--vance-paris-ai-summit-rejects-ai-safety-for-opportunity-excessive-regulation-could-kill-industry|Vance's Paris "opportunity not safety" agenda]].
