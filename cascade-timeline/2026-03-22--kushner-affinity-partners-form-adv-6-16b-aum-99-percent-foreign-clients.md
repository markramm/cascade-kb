# Kushner Affinity Partners Form ADV: $6.16B AUM, 99% Non-U.S. Clients

On **March 22, 2026**, Jared Kushner's private equity firm **Affinity Partners** filed its annual **Form ADV** with the SEC, disclosing:

- **$6.16 billion in assets under management**
- **99% of clients are non-U.S. persons**
- Primary capital sources include: Saudi Public Investment Fund ($2B commitment, June 2021); Qatar Investment Authority + Lunate/Abu Dhabi ($1.5B additional, closed December 2024); other Gulf-state sovereign vehicles

## The Conflict Structure

This disclosure is filed while Kushner serves as **Trump's informal foreign policy envoy** with an active role in:
- U.S.-Gulf state diplomatic and commercial negotiations
- U.S.-Ukraine/Russia ceasefire diplomacy
- U.S.-Israel policy coordination

The 99% foreign-client ratio means virtually all of Affinity Partners' fee revenue derives from the same foreign sovereign capital pools that Kushner simultaneously represents in policy contexts as an unofficial White House channel.
