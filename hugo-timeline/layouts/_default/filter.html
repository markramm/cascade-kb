How filtering works

Permalink semantics

Every filter is a URL.

When you change a filter, the URL updates. Copy it, paste it in an article, share it on social media — the page reproduces exactly when someone follows the link.

Combine fields.

Filter by actor, tag, lane, year range, status, or full-text search — all at once. Results update live.
