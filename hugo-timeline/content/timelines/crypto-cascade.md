In four years the United States went from prosecuting cryptocurrency executives to having the President's family operate a cryptocurrency platform. World Liberty Financial — owned majority by the Trump family — issued tokens whose appreciation directly enriched the President during his term. The SEC's enforcement priorities reversed within weeks of Trump 2.0. Pam Bondi shut down the DOJ KleptoCapture unit on day one.

This is not regulatory capture in the metaphorical sense. It is the literal operational use of the presidency to inflate a financial product owned by the President. The timeline below shows how it built.
