The Mueller report concluded that the Russian government interfered in the 2016 U.S. presidential election "in sweeping and systematic fashion." It did not establish criminal conspiracy with the Trump campaign — but it documented dozens of contacts between named Russian intelligence assets and named Trump-orbit operatives. Six American operatives were ultimately convicted; all of them were pardoned, dropped, or rehabilitated by 2026.

This timeline plots the documented interactions, indictments, pardons, and policy reversals. The pattern is not closure — it is institutionalization. Every prosecutorial intervention has been undone at higher altitude. Putin remains in power; Trump remains in power; the operational infrastructure remains operational.
