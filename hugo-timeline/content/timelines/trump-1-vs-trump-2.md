The 2017–2021 Trump administration was famously chaotic. Cabinet officials resigned or were fired at record rates. Many policy initiatives stalled because the personnel to execute them were missing or hostile.

The 2025–2026 administration is none of those things. Project 2025 spent four years pre-vetting personnel, drafting executive orders, and assembling agency restructuring plans. The same President is now executing a fully prepared program. This timeline shows both terms on the same axis — first-term chaos visible on the left, second-term velocity on the right. The pattern in the bottom rows (Vought, Patel, Bondi, Vance, DOGE) is the *staffing layer* that makes the difference.
