## Biography

Benjamin Netanyahu has served as Prime Minister of Israel for longer than any other person in the country's history, across multiple non-consecutive terms. His political survival has repeatedly depended on coalition partners drawn from the Israeli far right.

His government's surveillance apparatus is a recurring thread. Israeli spyware (Pegasus), built by NSO Group and staffed by alumni of the IDF's Unit 8200, has tracked Netanyahu's diplomacy: several European governments — among them Hungary and Poland — purchased Pegasus after meeting with him [TIER 2].

His government's conduct in Gaza following the October 7, 2023 Hamas attack became a significant fault line within the American left and a stress point in the broader two-Americas dynamic.
