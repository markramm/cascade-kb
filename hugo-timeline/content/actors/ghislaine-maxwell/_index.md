## Quick facts

Ghislaine Maxwell is a British former socialite, the daughter of media mogul Robert Maxwell, and the longtime associate, companion, and operational partner of financier Jeffrey Epstein. On December 29, 2021, a federal jury in the Southern District of New York convicted her on five of six counts, including sex trafficking of a minor; on June 28, 2022 she was sentenced to 20 years in prison. She is incarcerated at FPC Bryan, Texas, a minimum-security federal prison camp she was transferred to from FCI Tallahassee in August 2025.

## Biography

Ghislaine Maxwell was born December 25, 1961 in Maisons-Laffitte, France, the daughter of British media mogul Robert Maxwell, and was educated at Oxford. After her father's death, she relocated to New York and, by the early 1990s, had become the associate and companion of financier Jeffrey Epstein — a relationship that, according to the federal prosecution that convicted her, evolved into an operational partnership.

According to the federal case that ended in her conviction, Maxwell was a central recruiter and groomer of underage girls for Epstein's abuse network, with the prosecution presenting victim testimony describing systematic recruitment over roughly 1994–2004. On December 29, 2021, a Southern District of New York jury convicted her on five of six counts — including sex trafficking of a minor, and multiple conspiracy and transportation counts tied to enticing minors into illegal sex acts. On June 28, 2022 she was sentenced to 20 years in federal prison.

Maxwell's social connections were extensive, and she facilitated Epstein's access to elite networks in finance, politics, academia, and British royalty, including Prince Andrew. Her prosecution left unresolved the broader question of which of Epstein's documented associates were aware of, participated in, or were compromised by the abuse network. Separate allegations — for instance, claims by former Israeli intelligence officer Ari Ben-Menashe that Epstein and Maxwell ran a blackmail operation for Israeli intelligence — remain unproven; no U.S. court has found evidence of an intelligence operation, former Israeli Prime Minister Naftali Bennett publicly rejected the claim as "categorically and totally false" (July 2025), and no primary documents have been produced. Her father Robert Maxwell's own connections to Eastern Bloc intelligence are separately documented, but their extension to Ghislaine and Epstein remains unsubstantiated.
